Keto-ish Strawberry Jam

Keto-ish strawberry jam to enhance other baked goods!

This keto-ish strawberry jam is quick and easy to make and can be used in other recipes or to enhance other baked goods, such as your Crustless Keto Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. Strawberries
  • 1/4 Cup Erythritol, powdered
  • 2 Tbsp. Chia Seeds
  • 1/4 Cup Water

Instructions

  1. Cut Strawberries into cubes
  2. Place Strawberries into pot over medium heat
  3. Mix in Erythritol
  4. Allow Strawberry mixture to heat and release water
  5. Mash Strawberries to desired consistency using a potato masher or fork+spoon
  6. Allow the mixture to boil for approximately 5 minutes
  7. Add Water and Chia Seeds and stir well for 2 minutes
  8. Allow jam to cool to room temperature
  9. Pour jam into jar and refrigerate
