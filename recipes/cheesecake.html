Crustless Keto Cheesecake

A cheesecake for any skill level!

This simple Crustless Keto Cheesecake recipe is perfect for Keto-goers looking to dive into the world of delicious keto baked goods. It uses common ingredients and minimal steps to make it easy for anyone to achieve a great tasting cheesecake!

Ingredients

Base Layer

  • 16 oz. Cream Cheese, softened (2x 8 oz. Packages)
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Erythritol, powdered
  • 1 Tbsp. Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 tsp. Lemon Extract (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp. Orange Extract (optional)

Top Layer

  • 8 oz. Sour Cream
  • 3 Tbsp. Erythritol, powdered
  • 1 tsp. Vanilla Extract

Instructions

Base Layer

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F)
  2. Spray glass pie pan with non-stick oil
  3. Mix 16 oz. Cream Cheese until smooth
  4. Add 1/2 cup Erythritol and 1 Egg - mix untli smooth
  5. Add the remaining 3 Eggs, one at a time, whlie mixing
  6. Add Vanilla and optional Extracts and mix
  7. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes
  8. Allow base layer to slightly cool

Top Layer

  1. Mix 8 oz. Sour Cream, 3 Tbsp. Erythritol, and 1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
  2. Spread mixture on top of the base layer
  3. Bake for an additional 15 to 20 minutes
  4. Allow cheesecake to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate
